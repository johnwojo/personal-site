About

I'm a solutions engineer turned de facto product manager, with a background in building knowledge systems at scale. Most recently at Meta, I owned a ~3,000-asset Capacity library used across global infrastructure teams — acting as the connective tissue between engineering, tooling, and the people who needed answers fast.

Before that, I was part of the founding team at EliseAI, where I built the support function from the ground up. I think in systems, write to clarify, and tend to notice the organizational problems that nobody has officially assigned to anyone yet.

My longer arc is toward AI product and alignment work — the place where technical capability meets human consequence. I'm interested in what it means to build AI that's actually good for people, not just impressive to demo.
